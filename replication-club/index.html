COMING · THE REPLICATION CLUB

Run it yourself. We’ll keep the register.

Everything needed to rerun the battery is already public: the dataset, the scripts, the frozen scoring key. The Club adds the missing piece: a public register of who ran it, on what, and what they found.

The principle is reports, not telemetry: you run the instrument wherever and however you like, and if you choose to file what you found, it goes on the register with your name and your numbers, agreeing with ours or not. Disagreement is the more valuable filing.

The register charter publishes with the second battery’s paper, so that independent runs and the original share one format and one standard of evidence.

Start today if you like: the Evidence Room links the full dataset and code. The register will be waiting.

The bridge asks all who cross to keep their own step.
No tracking, no cookies, nothing recorded.
