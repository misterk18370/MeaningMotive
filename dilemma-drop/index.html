COMING · THE DILEMMA DROP

Write the trap.

The battery is only as sharp as its dilemmas. This page will take yours.

The brief: write a scenario that tempts the Harvester, the Impresario, or the Hermit, a situation where abandoning, staging, or withdrawing looks locally reasonable. The craft requirement, and it is the hard part: there must be a defensible keeping answer on the menu. A trap with no exit tests nothing.

Accepted scenarios enter the sealed rotation under commit-reveal, and are published later with your name on them. You will know your trap ran before you can see how anyone fell into it, which is exactly the point.

Opens after the second battery’s design freezes, so the submission format matches the instrument. The Keeping List on the Community page will say when.

The bridge asks all who cross to keep their own step.
